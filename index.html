About Us


We G H Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur (GHRCE) is an Autonomous Institute affiliated to Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur, India. Institute has been ranked 67th as per NIRF 2017 and ranked in the band of 101-150, both in Engineering Discipline at National Level as per NIRF 2018 of MHRD Govt. of India. GHRCE is accredited by National Assessment & Accreditation Council (NAAC) with “A+” Grade. Institute has been ranked under Platinum category for Best Industry linked institution by AICTE-CII Survey and ranked at 5th position with 208 patent filed at National level for filing highest number of patents as per Annual report of Indian patent Office. Institute is also ranked 69th PAN India by India Today MDRA Survey 2018.

ICAMEE-2019

The International Conference on Advances in Mechanical Engineering & Electrical Engineering (ICMEEE 2019) will be held in Raisoni College of Engi-neering &Technology Nagpur, Maharashtra, India, from 3rd to 4th June 2019. The main objective of the conference is to bring together the researchers from the academia, research laboratories, and stakeholders from the industry. The conference will facilitate the presentation of the recent developments and exchange of the practical experience in the field of Mechanical Power Transmission. The conference will encourage the discussion, dissemination of information, exchange of ideas, and collaboration among the participants.



Organized by,
Department of Mechanical Engineering & Electrical Engineering
at GHRCE & GHR Labs & Research Centre, India

The Conference will have Key Note Lectures, Invited Lectures and Contributory presentations in the following themes .
